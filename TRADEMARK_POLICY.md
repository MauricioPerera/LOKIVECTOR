# LokiVector Trademark Policy

**Effective Date:** 2025-12-06

## Overview

"LokiVector" is a trademark owned by LokiVector Contributors. This policy describes how you may and may not use the LokiVector trademark.

---

## Permitted Uses

You may use the LokiVector trademark in the following ways without explicit permission:

### 1. Referential Use
- Refer to LokiVector by name in articles, blog posts, documentation, or presentations
- Use the trademark to accurately describe that you are using LokiVector software
- Example: "Our application uses LokiVector for vector search"

### 2. Open Source Projects
- Use the trademark in open source projects that use LokiVector (MIT features only)
- Include attribution: "Powered by LokiVector"
- Link back to the official LokiVector repository

### 3. Educational Use
- Use the trademark in educational materials, courses, or tutorials
- Reference LokiVector in academic papers or research

### 4. Community Use
- Use the trademark in community forums, discussions, or support channels
- Share screenshots or demos that include the LokiVector name

---

## Restricted Uses

You may **NOT** use the LokiVector trademark in the following ways without explicit written permission:

### 1. Product or Service Names
- ❌ Use "LokiVector" as part of your product or service name
- ❌ Create variations like "LokiVector Pro", "LokiVector Cloud", "LokiVector Enterprise" (unless licensed)
- ❌ Use "LokiVector" in domain names for commercial purposes

### 2. Commercial Branding
- ❌ Use the trademark in a way that suggests endorsement or partnership
- ❌ Use the trademark in advertising or marketing materials without permission
- ❌ Create logos or visual representations that incorporate the trademark

### 3. Competitive Products
- ❌ Use the trademark to promote competing products
- ❌ Use the trademark in a way that creates confusion about product origin
- ❌ Suggest that your product is "based on" or "forked from" LokiVector without clear attribution

### 4. Commercial Services
- ❌ Use the trademark to brand commercial services (SaaS, hosting, consulting)
- ❌ Use the trademark in commercial licensing or distribution agreements
- ❌ Use the trademark in investor materials or business plans without permission

---

## Commercial License Holders

If you hold a Commercial License:

- ✅ You may use the trademark in connection with your licensed use
- ✅ You may state that you are a "LokiVector Commercial License Holder"
- ✅ You may use the trademark in marketing materials for your licensed deployment
- ❌ You may not use the trademark to suggest you are the owner or official distributor
- ❌ You may not sublicense trademark use to third parties

---

## Attribution Requirements

When using the LokiVector trademark, you must:

1. **Use the full name** - "LokiVector" (not abbreviated or modified)
2. **Include proper attribution** - Link to the official repository or website
3. **Use appropriate symbols** - "LokiVector™" or "LokiVector" (trademark symbol when appropriate)
4. **Maintain accuracy** - Do not misrepresent your relationship with LokiVector

---

## Logo and Visual Identity

The LokiVector logo and visual identity are protected:

- ❌ Do not use the logo without permission
- ❌ Do not create derivative logos
- ❌ Do not incorporate the logo into your own branding
- ✅ You may link to official LokiVector resources that display the logo

---

## Domain Names

You may **NOT** register domain names that:

- Include "lokivector" in a way that suggests official affiliation
- Create confusion about the source of LokiVector
- Compete with official LokiVector domains

Examples of prohibited domains:
- ❌ lokivector-cloud.com
- ❌ lokivector-pro.com
- ❌ lokivector-enterprise.com
- ❌ lokivector-saas.com

## Enforcement

LokiVector will take appropriate action to protect its trademark, including:

- Sending cease and desist notices
- Filing trademark infringement claims
- Seeking damages for unauthorized use

We prefer to resolve issues amicably and will typically contact you first before taking legal action.

---

## Fair Use

This policy does not restrict fair use of the trademark for:

- News reporting
- Commentary and criticism
- Parody (when clearly identified as such)
- Comparative advertising (when accurate and not misleading)
